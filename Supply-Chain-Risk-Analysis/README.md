# Global Supply Chain Risk Analysis

This project analyzes global supply chain operations with a focus on identifying risks, disruptions, and performance patterns using data-driven insights and dashboards.

## 📊 Project Overview
The analysis covers:
- Shipment distribution across transport modes
- Disruption rates and operational risks
- Lead time evaluation for deliveries
- Impact of weather on logistics performance
- Product category distribution

## 📁 Dataset Details
The dataset includes:
- Shipment ID, Date, Origin, Destination
- Transport Mode (Air, Rail, Road, Sea)
- Lead Time, Distance, Weight
- Carrier Reliability and Fuel Cost
- Disruption Status and Risk Score
- Weather Conditions and Time factors

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- Shipment distribution by transport mode
- Average lead time analysis
- Disruption rate visualization
- Weather impact on shipments
- Product category share
- KPI cards for overall performance

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Air transport provides the fastest delivery times (~2 days)
- Sea transport has the longest delays (~15 days)
- High disruption rate (~61%) indicates operational risk
- Weather conditions significantly affect delivery performance
- Product distribution is balanced across categories

## 💡 Recommendations
- Reduce disruptions through proactive planning
- Optimize transport mix to balance speed and cost
- Incorporate weather data into logistics planning
- Continuously monitor performance using dashboards

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Analysis & Visualization

## 🎯 Objective
To analyze supply chain risks and improve logistics efficiency through data-driven decision-making.
